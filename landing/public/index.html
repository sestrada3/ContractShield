Risk Score & Verdict

Every contract gets a 1–10 fairness score with a plain-English verdict. Instantly know if you're looking at a standard deal or something to push back on.

  • Animated score ring shows risk at a glance
  • Color-coded: green / amber / red
  • Benchmarked against real contracts in your category

Clause-by-Clause Analysis

Every clause is extracted, rated HIGH / MEDIUM / FAVORABLE, and explained in plain English with the original legal text alongside for reference.

  • Automatic identification of non-competes, IP grabs, auto-renewals
  • Market benchmark on every clause
  • One-tap expand to see the full original text

"What to Say" — Negotiation Scripts

This is the feature that sets ContractShield apart. Every red-flag clause comes with a copy-ready negotiation script — the exact words to say to get better terms. No guesswork. No awkward silence.

  • Word-for-word language you can use in an email or call
  • Calibrated to be firm but professional
  • One-tap copy to clipboard

Key Dates & Deadlines

ContractShield extracts every important date — opt-out windows, renewal deadlines, notice periods — and flags them by urgency so nothing slips past you.

  • Color-coded urgency: high / medium / low
  • Plain-English description of each deadline
  • Action steps for each date

Privacy-First by Design

Your contract is analyzed and immediately discarded. PII is automatically stripped before processing. Your sensitive documents are never stored on our servers.

  • Encrypted in transit (TLS)
  • SSNs, emails, phone numbers auto-redacted
  • Only your results are saved — never the document
